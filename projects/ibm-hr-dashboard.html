An interactive Tableau dashboard exploring IBM's HR dataset — surfacing patterns in employee attrition, demographics, job satisfaction, and compensation. Built to turn raw workforce data into clear, actionable insights for HR decision-makers.

Live Dashboard
Key Findings

What the data shows

Attrition Rate
~16%
Overall attrition rate in the dataset, highest among Sales Representatives and Lab Technicians.
Satisfaction Driver
WLB
Work-life balance emerges as the strongest predictor of job satisfaction across all departments.
Tenure Risk
0–2 yrs
Employees in their first two years are significantly more likely to leave than longer-tenured staff.
Methodology

How it was built

The IBM HR Analytics dataset contains 1,470 employee records across 35 variables. Data was cleaned and structured in Tableau Prep before connecting to Tableau Desktop. Custom calculated fields were used to derive attrition segments, satisfaction composites, and compensation bands. The final dashboard uses a coordinated filter system so every chart updates from a single click.
