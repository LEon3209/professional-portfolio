Excel · Data Viz · Analytics

Bike Sales
Dashboard

Nov 2025 · Microsoft Excel

An interactive Excel dashboard analyzing bike purchase behavior across demographics — income levels, commute distance, age brackets, and regional trends. Built with pivot tables, slicers, and dynamic charts for self-serve exploration of the data.

Excel Data Viz Analytics
Dashboard Preview
Key Findings

What the data shows

Top Buyer
Mid-age
Adults aged 31–54 are the most frequent bike purchasers, outpacing both younger and older segments.
Income Effect
+$10K
Buyers earn on average ~$10,000 more than non-buyers, suggesting income as a key purchase driver.
Commute Sweet Spot
0–1 mi
Short-distance commuters (under 1 mile) show the highest purchase rates — bikes as practical transport.
Methodology

How it was built

Raw sales data was cleaned in Excel — removing duplicates, standardizing categorical fields (marital status, region, education), and bucketing ages into meaningful brackets. Pivot tables aggregate the data across multiple dimensions, feeding directly into the chart series. Slicers are connected across all pivots so a single click filters the entire dashboard simultaneously.
