Methodology

From data to model

The project starts from a manually cleaned governance dataset. The exploratory phase profiles the distributions of member attributes — age, tenure, gender, and educational background — and the correlations between them, comparing how those distributions differ across Boards of Directors and Executive Committees. A linear-regression model then quantifies the relationships, with the two notebooks (EDA and regression) kept separate for clarity and reproducibility.
